Empowering Student Careers

Our Four-Star Program pairs students with mentors for six weeks to explore career choices. We activate student agency and ownership of their career journey.

By helping one student at a time, we address the underemployment problem facing new college graduates.

Additional Volunteering Opportunities

Spotlight

Share your career story to help inspire students. After you answer a few questions, we'll send you a draft to approve and then publish your story on our blog. To start the process, fill out this form.

Coverage

Substitutes provide flexibilty for career mentors and consistency for students. On the occasion a career mentor cannot make session, they complete a coverage requests. The coverage requests are available on the Foundation Portal for substitutes to claim. Once a specific session is claimed, the sub will receive the meeting invite which contains session details.
