About Us

Who we are

The Sujeeth Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to unlocking the opportunity of education.

What We Do

We help students network into their first job through a six-week mentorship program that encourages students to exercise agency in their career.

Why We Do It

Academic institutions are places where experimentation thrives and opportunities to grow are abundant. We want to unlock that value for every student.
