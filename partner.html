The Career Circle Approach

Six-Week Mentorship

We pair mentors with groups of six students in career circles, creating a supportive environment for career exploration.

Student Agency

Our program encourages students to take ownership of their career paths through guided exploration and skill development.

Institutional Partnership

We work with educational institutions to build structure, recruit volunteers, and fund circles that can scale from one class to the entire school.

Forming Career Circles

Create Student Groups

Partner institutions form career circles of six students each, providing a collaborative learning environment.

Establish Meeting Times

Each circle determines a weekly meeting schedule that accommodates both students and mentors.

Introduce Mentors

Student meet with mentors who will facilitate weekly sessions.
